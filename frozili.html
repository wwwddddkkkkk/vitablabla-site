What it is

A slow-melting little reset, in a tin you'll keep.

Frozili is a hard candy with a cooling center, built around a cold-brew coffee reduction. It is small, it is icy, and it stays in your mouth for a generous seven minutes — long enough to mark the end of a busy block.

We made it for the moment between two long emails, on a flight, halfway through a study session. Not a meal. Not a coffee. Just a comma.

The story

43 prototypes. One slow melt.

Frozili started as a question pinned over a workbench: what's the smallest possible thing that can mark the end of a 3 p.m. dip?

Forty-three formulations later, we landed on something specific: cold-brew coffee, an icy slow-release center, and a tin small enough to live in your pocket.
