The story

We love to travel — and we wanted every fruit, everywhere.

The brief was simple. We wanted to enjoy mango in a winter airport, lychee on a long drive, strawberries on a Tuesday morning meeting. We wanted color, real flavor, and a snack we could actually carry.

Freeze-drying turned out to be the answer. Slow, careful, and remarkably honest — it pulls every drop of water out and leaves the fruit's color, flavor, and a kind of airy crunch you cannot fake. Real fruit, lighter to carry, ready when you are.

That is OhCrisp. A small pouch, big crunch, and a brighter way to snack — wherever you happen to be that day.

The story

A slow vacuum, and an honest piece of fruit.

OhCrisp is freeze-dried fruit, made the long way. Whole, ripe fruit goes in. Water comes out — slowly, under vacuum, with no heat to bruise the flavor.

What is left is the fruit, exactly as it was picked, with a kind of airy crunch you cannot fake. No oil. No sugar. No anything else.
