Quick questions

Why is coffee candy good for lake days?

Coffee candy for lake days gives you the coffee flavour and a cooling finish without brewing, carrying, or keeping a drink cold. A slow-melting piece needs one dry second to unwrap, holds its shape in a warm bag, and lasts about the length of a sunbathe between swims — no kettle, no cooler space, and nothing to spill off the dock.

Is coffee candy better than iced coffee at a lake?

For a light lift, usually yes. Iced coffee waters down within twenty minutes on a warm dock and takes up cooler room you'd rather give to real drinks, while a coffee candy is a self-contained coffee moment that survives heat and splash. Iced coffee still wins if you actually want the volume of a full drink.

Will coffee candy melt in a warm lake bag?

No. A slow-melt coffee candy holds its shape, so individually wrapped pieces keep fine in a sun-warmed tote through a full day. Keep the tin out of direct sun and away from wet towels and it stays firm from the first swim to the drive home.
