Why the movie theater asks for a calmer kind of candy.

Many sweets are built for brightness: crunch, chew, sourness, wrappers that announce themselves. A theater is the opposite environment. You are sitting still. The room is shared. The point is immersion. That is why coffee flavored candy lands so differently here than fruit chews or sharp mints. It tastes adult, slightly toasted, and already in conversation with the smell of popcorn in the air.

If you have ever liked the logic behind alternatives to basic mints, the theater version makes the case even more clearly. Refreshment matters, but social volume matters too.

A non-gum refresher works better when you are not trying to chew through the plot.
