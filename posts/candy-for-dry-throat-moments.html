Why “dry throat moments” happen in offices, cars, and planes.

It’s rarely dramatic. It’s just the combination of talking, dry air, coffee, and the way people forget to drink water when they’re focused. The mouth gets busy, the room gets cold, and suddenly everything feels a little scratchy. That’s the moment people reach for gum, mints, or coffee candy — not to eat, exactly, but to edit the sensation.
