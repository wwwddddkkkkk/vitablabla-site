How to keep freeze-dried fruit crisp: the humidity rule in plain language.

Freeze-dried fruit is shelf-stable, but it hates moisture. Night snack setups tend to be humid — steaming tea, open dishwasher, warm kitchens. Keep the bag sealed, pour a little into a dish, and close it again. Treat it like something delicate and it stays bright.
