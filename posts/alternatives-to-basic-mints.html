Why basic mints stop working for some people: too sharp, too instant.

Mints are built like exclamation points. That is useful when you want a hard reset, but less useful when you just want your mouth to feel cleaner before a call, after lunch, or between errands. The issue is not whether mint is “good.” It is whether it matches the tone of the moment.
