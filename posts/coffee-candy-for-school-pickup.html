Quick questions

Is coffee candy a good pick-me-up for the school pickup line?

Yes. It delivers coffee flavour and a cooling finish with no cup to spill, no queue to join, and no cleanup, which suits a few parked minutes in the car when making or buying a real coffee isn't practical.

Will coffee candy keep me awake at night if I have one at three?

Treat it as a flavour-and-refresh moment rather than a caffeine source. The coffee taste and cool finish help you feel reset, so a mid-afternoon piece is far less likely to disturb your evening sleep than a second full cup of coffee.

Why choose coffee candy over gum or mints on the school run?

Gum is restless and easy to drop in a busy car, and mint can feel sharp at the end of a tiring day. Slow-melting iced coffee candy is quiet and hands-free — coffee first, then a clean cooling finish — so it refreshes without chewing, crinkling, or a strong menthol hit before the kids pile in.
