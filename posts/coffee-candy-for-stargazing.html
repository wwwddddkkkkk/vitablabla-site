What to pack for a night under the sky.

A good stargazing kit stays deliberately small so nothing interrupts the quiet:

  • A blanket that holds the day's warmth — wool or fleece, laid over grass that's already cooling.
  • A red-light torch — red doesn't reset your night vision the way white light does.
  • A tin of slow-melt coffee candy — one piece per faint-star wait, no cup, no crumbs, no glow.
  • Something warm to drink for later — for after your eyes have had their fill.
  • A star map or an offline app — screen dimmed all the way down, or better, on red mode.
