Quick questions

Is coffee candy a good idea first thing in the morning?

Yes, as a bridge rather than a substitute. A piece of coffee candy gives you the taste and small ritual of coffee in the minutes before your first cup is brewed, so an early morning starts with flavour instead of an empty wait. It pairs with your real coffee rather than replacing it.

Does coffee candy actually wake you up?

It's better thought of as a sensory cue than a caffeine hit. The coffee flavour and cooling finish of an icy coffee candy act as a small wake-up signal — a refreshing nudge that the day has started — while your brewed cup does the heavier lifting.

How long does a piece of slow-melting coffee candy last?

A slow-melt coffee candy is designed to dissolve gradually rather than be chewed, so a single piece can last several unhurried minutes — roughly long enough to cover the brew time of a pour-over or the first stretch of a morning commute.
