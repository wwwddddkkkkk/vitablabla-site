What makes a candy moving-day-proof.

Not every sweet survives a chaotic day of lifting. A moving-day-friendly candy tends to share a short list of traits:

  • No-kitchen: it works with everything packed — no kettle, plug, or clean mug needed.
  • One-handed: it opens and eats while the other hand steadies a box.
  • Pocket-sized: small enough to ride in a jacket while you climb stairs.
  • Heat-steady: it won't melt into a mess in a warm pocket the way chocolate would.
  • Slow to finish: one piece stretches across several trips instead of one bite.

Seen together, those traits describe a very specific small reset — one designed for a day with no working kitchen and no spare hands.
