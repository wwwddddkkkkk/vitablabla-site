Why is coffee candy good for road trips?

Coffee candy for road trips gives you the coffee flavour and a cooling refresh without a drive-thru detour or a hot cup sliding around the car. Is coffee candy better than gas-station coffee on a long drive?

They do different jobs. Gas-station coffee means an exit, a line, and a cup that waters down and cools before the next town; a coffee candy is a small, self-contained coffee moment with no stop, no spill, and no crash afterward. For a light lift in the long middle of a drive, the candy is usually the easier call.

Will coffee candy melt in a hot car?

No. A slow-melt coffee candy is made to hold its shape, so individually wrapped pieces come through a warm car fine. Keep the tin in the glovebox or a door pocket rather than on the dashboard in direct sun, and it stays firm from the first mile to the last.
