About / Vitablabla

We build playful premium snacks for everyday little moments.

What we are

A modern snack brand house creating products that are easy to love and hard to forget.

Where we're based

Started in a Yale kitchen during late-night study sessions. Now based in Jersey City, still a small studio with a shared belief that snacks deserve a little more thought.

Studio · started at Yale, now in Jersey City Drops 1 — 5 / Snack House

The short version.

Vitablabla is a modern snack brand house creating treats with distinct flavors, memorable textures, and strong visual identities.

"Our first product, Frozili, is an icy cool coffee candy: a slow-melting little reset with coffee flavor and icy freshness."

It was created for desk days, long drives, late nights, after meals, and all the small moments when you want something refreshing but don't want another drink.

Across every product we build, we care about one thing: making snacks feel more memorable. Icy, crunchy, colorful, slow-melting, surprising, soothing, or oddly satisfying — every Vitablabla brand starts with a sensory idea worth remembering.
