About / Vitablabla

Small, thoughtful products for specific tastes.

What we are

A home for small, thoughtful products made to feel personal, delightful, and worth sharing.

Where we're based

Started in a Yale kitchen during late-night study sessions. Now based in Bethesda, MD, still guided by the belief that everyday products deserve a little more thought.

The short version.

Vitablabla is a home for small, thoughtful products made for people with specific tastes. We create everyday brands around real cravings, clear product ideas, and playful details — from crunchy fruit snacks to refreshing little rituals.

"What is a small desire that deserves a better product?"

We believe the best products should feel personal, well-made, and easy to love from the first try.

Each Vitablabla brand starts with that simple question, then becomes something specific: a texture, a flavor, a ritual, and a set of little details people naturally want to share.
