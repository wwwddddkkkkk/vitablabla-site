Frequently asked

Quick answers, first.

Most messages we get fall into one of these. If yours is here, you might not need to write us at all — but please do anyway.

Where can I buy Frozili or OhCrisp?

+

Both Frozili and OhCrisp are available on TikTok Shop and on our brand website. Head to the Frozili or OhCrisp page to grab the latest pack.

Do you work with creators?

+

Yes — and we keep things small and intentional. If you'd like to work together, send us a quick note at info@vitablabla.com with a link or two to your work. We respond to everyone.

I'm a buyer at a retail store. Where do I start?

+

Email info@vitablabla.com with the subject "Wholesale" and we'll reply with wholesale details, current availability, and the best next step.

I had a bad piece. Can you help?

+

Yes, please. Email support@vitablabla.com with a photo and your batch code (printed on the bottom of every Frozili tin) and we'll get a replacement out to you the same day.

Are you hiring?

+

We hire slowly and only when we can do it well. Open roles always live on this site. If nothing is listed, you can still send a portfolio to info@vitablabla.com — we keep good ones on file.
